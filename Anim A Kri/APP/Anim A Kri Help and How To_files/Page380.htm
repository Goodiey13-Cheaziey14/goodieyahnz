    ANIM A KRI ANIMATION

 

ANIMATION IS EASY, BUT WITH CODE WHICH IS ALREADY WRITTEN OUT FOR YOU. THE ANIMATION IS 18 FPS (FRAMES PER SECOND)

 

                         

                

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

WRITE THE URL IN BETWEEN THE QUOTATION MARKS. THE REST OF THE CODE DOES NOT MATTER BUT DO NOT EDIT THEM. IN CASE YOU DON’T KNOW IMAGE URLs, CLICK THIS GET HERE BUTTON       

IT WILL TAKE YOU TO “C:/USERS/”.

THEN YOU CLICK YOUR USERNAME. THIS USERNAME IS THE FIRST NAME SHOWN WHEN YOU BOOT YOUR DEVICE. THEN CLICK PICTURES OR ANY OTHER FOLDER and open a picture OF YOUR CHOICE. ON THE URL BAR OF YOUR BROWSER, COPY THE IMAGE URL USING CTRL+C OR RIGHT CLICK AND CLICK COPY. THEN PASTE BETWEEN THE QUOTATION MARKS {“”} USING CTRL+V OR RIGHT CLICK AND CLICK PASTE
